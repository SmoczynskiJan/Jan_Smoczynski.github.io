About Me

Hi there! I’m Jan Smoczyński, a web development enthusiast beginning my journey. By day, I’m a junior support specialist; by night, I dive into HTML, CSS, and the art of making the web come alive.

I’m captivated by design that’s not just beautiful but meaningful and sleek layouts that are as useful as they are emotive. Creating something that speaks through its elegance and functionality drives my curiosity and fuels my love for coding.

I’m inspired by design that seamlessly blends beauty and purpose. Finding layouts that tell a story fuels my passion for coding. Having nearly completed a full-stack course, I’m excited to build my first web app and see where this journey takes me.

Beyond the screen, I enjoy bouldering, woodworking, and perfecting details, whether in code or physical objects. This site is my creative playground, where I share ideas, growth, and joy in coding. I hope it inspires you to explore, create, and connect.

Thanks for stopping by. I’d love to hear from you!
